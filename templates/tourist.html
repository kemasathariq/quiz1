A Simple Guide to Jakarta

Explore the heart of Indonesia with this mini-guide to some of Jakarta's most iconic and must-visit attractions.

Start at Monas (National Monument)

Begin your journey at the iconic 132-meter tower in the center of Merdeka Square, symbolizing the fight for Indonesian independence.

Witness Harmony at Istiqlal & The Cathedral

Just a short walk from Monas, visit Southeast Asia's largest mosque and the beautiful neo-gothic Jakarta Cathedral, standing side-by-side.

Step Back in Time at Kota Tua

Explore Jakarta's Old Town, the historic heart of the city, with its Dutch colonial architecture, museums, and charming Fatahillah Square.

Culinary Delights & City Lights at Blok M

Conclude your day with vibrant street food, cozy cafes, and panoramic views of Jakarta's sparkling cityscape in the Blok M area.
