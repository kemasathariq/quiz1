My Hometown: Jakarta

Jakarta, the sprawling capital of Indonesia, is a dynamic and vibrant metropolis that serves as the nation's economic, cultural, and political heart. Known as a city that never sleeps, it's a place of incredible contrasts, where towering modern skyscrapers stand alongside charming historical districts.

As a melting pot of cultures, Jakarta offers an unparalleled culinary scene and a rich tapestry of traditions. From the historic colonial buildings of Kota Tua to the iconic Bundaran HI roundabout, the city is a testament to Indonesia's rich history and its ambitious future.
