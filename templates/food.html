Local Food of Jakarta

A culinary journey through the iconic and delicious street food and traditional dishes of the Betawi culture. Hover over any image to learn more.

Kerak Telor

A spicy omelette made from glutinous rice, fried with egg and served with fried shallots and dried shrimp.

Soto Betawi

A rich and creamy beef soup, slow-cooked in a coconut milk broth with potatoes and tomatoes.

Nasi Uduk

A fragrant rice dish cooked in coconut milk, pandan, and lemongrass, served with various side dishes.

Gado-Gado

An Indonesian salad of boiled vegetables, egg, tofu, and lontong, all dressed in a rich peanut sauce.

Ketoprak

A vegetarian dish of tofu, rice vermicelli, and bean sprouts, covered in a sweet and savory peanut sauce.

Asinan Betawi

A dish of pickled and preserved vegetables, served in a tangy and spicy vinegar and peanut sauce.
